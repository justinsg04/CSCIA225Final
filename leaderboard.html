Welcome to the N & J Matching Game! Here's how to play:
Click "Play" then select difficulty level. Once selected, click "Start Game"
- If you selected "Easy," you will be given a 4x4 grid and will NOT be timed.
- If you selected "Medium," you will be given a 6x6 grid will have 5 minutes to match all the cards.
- If you selected "Hard," you will be given a 8x8 grid and will have 7 minutes to match all the cards.
If you successfully match all cards, your time taken will be recorded and submitted to the leaderboard.
